# GD GPT
- This mod uses Google Gemini to bring **AI** to Geometry Dash.

# How to use
- First, you need a **[Gemini API key](https://aistudio.google.com/api-keys)**.
- Next, put it into the mod settings.
- Finally, enjoy your AI! :)
